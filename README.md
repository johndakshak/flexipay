A modern, user-friendly Wallet and Savings app that helps users save, transfer, and manage their money effortlessly. Designed for flexible goal-based saving and peer-to-peer transactions.

## Value Proposition Statement

FlexiPay helps young, everyday Nigerians save smarter and manage money effortlessly by combining a simple wallet, flexible goal-based savings, and fast peer-to-peer transfers in one playful, easy-to-use app. Unlike traditional fintech apps that feel rigid or complicated, FlexiPay makes saving fun, visual, and social ‚Äî allowing users to create multiple savings goals, track progress clearly, and move money seamlessly between wallet and savings. Designed to be lightweight, transparent, and youth-friendly, FlexiPay empowers individuals to stay disciplined, reach personal goals faster, and enjoy complete control over their finances.

## Features ‚úÖ

- Create a personal wallet with a secure balance
- Fund wallet via mobile money or bank transfer
- Withdraw funds to your bank account
- Transfer money to other FlexiPay users
- Create goal-based savings plans
- Deposit into or withdraw from savings goals
- View transaction history for wallet and savings
- Simple, playful, and mobile-friendly design
